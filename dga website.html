VECTOR ILLUSTRATIONS

As Created with Adobe Illustrator

Vector illustrations are preferred by the industry
due to their scalability and lack of loss of resolution,
which allows a vast array of applications and uses.

The user positions a series of dots and connecting lines known as points and paths,
which together make up the final graphic. special attention must be given to
ensure proper alignmnet and connections of the shapes in favour of clean work.

  • Construct your shapes
